BroadwayWorld first reported the news this morning.He was nominated for an Academy Award for Best Supporting Actor for his portrayal of pilot Chuck Yeager in \u201cThe Right Stuff.\u201d He was also\u00a0the\u00a0author of forty-four plays, as well as several books, including short stories, essays, and memoirs. He received the Pulitzer Prize for Drama in 1979 for his play \u201cBuried Child.\u201dAs BroadwayWorld notes, \u201cShepard\u2019s plays are chiefly known for their bleak, poetic, often surrealist elements, black humor and rootless characters living on the outskirts of American society.\u201dIn 2009, he\u00a0received the PEN/Laura Pels International Foundation for Theater Award as a master American dramatist.\u00a0Shepard was elected to The American Academy of Arts and Letters in 1986.
