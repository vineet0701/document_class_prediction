All of that comes in a phone whose pre-orders start at $1,195. Up to now, RED has only released one obscured teaser image of the device. But the company recently gave popular YouTube tech reviewer Marques Brownlee - also known as \" MKBHD \" - a non-functional prototype of what it says the Hydrogen One will ultimately look like.
